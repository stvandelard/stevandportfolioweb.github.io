UEFA Champions League Fantasy Game

A UEFA Champions League Simulation CLI-based Video Game. UEFA Champions League is an annual club association football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). It is contested by top-division European clubs, deciding the competition winners through a round robin group stage to qualify for a double-legged knockout format, and a single leg final. There are 32 teams are drawn into eight groups of four teams and play each other in a double round-robin system. The eight group winners and eight runners-up proceed to the knockout phase that culminates with the final match.
In this game, there are 64 teams from across Europe the players can select. Once the players selected 1 team, they can change the manager name or not and then they can start the tournament simulation. First, the game will select 32 (including the team that the player selected before) teams out of 64 teams randomly, and put it on 8 groups. Each group had 4 teams on it, this step is called "drawing". After the group stage drawing, players will be directed to the "main page", which consists of 5 features: 1.Advance, 2.Data, 3.Stats, 4.Restart Game, 5.Exit Game. If the player choose Data they will able to see all the information of 32 participating teams including club name, country, home stadium, manager, the team's UCL titles and which group they are drawn. If the player choose Stats, they will able to see the player's team stats, other teams stats and match stats. If the player choose Advance, the game will simulate the group stage matchday 1. The game will randomly simulate the match score and automatically updated the group stage standings. After 6 matchday group stage matches, if the players ranked 1st or 2nd in the group stage standing, the player will advance to the double-legged knockout stage, otherwise the player will be eliminated and the game is over.
In the knockout stage, each stage will be randomly drawn to determine which team the player will face. The knockout stage format will be played in 2 legs, there are several conditions for each team to win and advance: by aggregate, away goals, or penalties. This format is applied for Round of 16, Quarter Finals and Semifinal. The final itself will be played in a single match to determine the UEFA Champions League Winner.
This game was developed using pascal programming language with 3006 lines of source code. Sublime Text was the main IDE used for developing this video game.
